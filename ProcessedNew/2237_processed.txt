sequence analysis htseqtools: high-throughput sequencing quality control, processing and visualization in r we provide a bioconductor package with quality assessment, processing and visualization tools for high-throughput sequencing data, with emphasis in chip-seq and rna-seq studies. it includes detection of outliers and biases, inefficient immuno-precipitation and overamplification artifacts, de novo identification of read-rich genomic regions and visualization of the location and coverage of genomic region lists.
